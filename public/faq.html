Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gestura?

−

Gestura is an interactive platform designed to help individuals learn and communicate through sign language. It translates sign language into text and provides videos to support learning.

How does the translation work?

+

To translate sign language, you simply upload a video where the sign language gesture is performed. Our system uses machine learning to predict the meaning of the gesture and displays the translation in text form

Is there a mobile app available?

+

Currently, there is no mobile app available for Gestura. However, you can access all features directly through our website.

How do I upload a video for translation?

+

Click on the “Upload” button, select your video file, and it will be processed by our machine learning model. The predicted translation will be displayed after processing.

What happens to the video after I upload it?

+

Once the video is uploaded, it is processed to identify the sign language gesture and provide the translated text. The video and the translation are stored in your history for future reference.

Do I need to log in to use the website?

+

You don’t need to log in to upload videos, but you’ll need to register an account if you want to save your history or access personalized features.

How can I view past translations?

+

You can view your past translations by visiting the “History” page, where all your previous uploaded videos and their translations are stored.

Can I learn sign language here?

+

Yes! The “Learn” page offers a dictionary where you can look up words and view the corresponding sign language gesture video.

Can I delete videos from my history?

+

Yes, you can delete videos from your history at any time by clicking on the delete button next to the video.
